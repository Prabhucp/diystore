Affordable Electronic Components


DIYStore India is a young business with a goal of enabling the 'MAKER MOVEMENT' in India. Our primary aim is to provide common semiconductors and related items at the correct price.



We ourselves use the products we sell and DIYStore was launched because we found it hard to source semiconductors in India via mail order at the right prices. Of course one could always buy the items from China via AxxExpress, TxxBxx or similar websites but we found the lead times to be detrimental to quick-turnaround prototyping.
